Fishball

Fishballs are a popular street food in the Philippines, made from finely ground fish meat mixed with flour and seasonings, then shaped into small balls and deep-fried until golden and crispy. They are often served with a variety of dipping sauces, making them a delicious and affordable snack enjoyed by many.


Price: P30



Kwek Kwek

Kwek-Kwek is a popular Filipino street food made of quail eggs coated in an orange batter and deep-fried until crispy. It's often served with a tangy vinegar dipping sauce, making it a delicious and addictive snack enjoyed by many.


Price: P50



Gulaman

Gulaman is a popular Filipino dessert made from agar-agar, a gelatinous substance derived from seaweed. It is often flavored with fruit juices or sweetened with sugar, and can be served in various forms such as cubes, strips, or even as a jelly-like consistency. Gulaman is commonly enjoyed as a refreshing treat, especially during hot weather, and is often used as a base for other desserts like halo-halo or as a topping for shaved ice.


Price: P20



Dragon Balls

Dragon Balls are a popular snack in the Philippines, made from a mixture of ground pork, shrimp, and vegetables, shaped into small balls and deep-fried until golden and crispy. They are often served with a sweet and tangy dipping sauce, making them a delicious and satisfying treat enjoyed by many.


Price: P100
